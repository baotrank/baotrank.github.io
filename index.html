About

Hello!

I'm Tran K, a Junior Data Analyst with a diverse background in sales and a newfound passion for unraveling insights from data. My journey into the realm of data analysis has been shaped by my experiences as a senior sales associate, where I developed a deep understanding of customer behavior and the intricacies of sales processes.

In my previous role, I honed my interpersonal skills and customer-centric approach while leading sales initiatives, nurturing client relationships, and exceeding sales targets. Through these experiences, I gained invaluable insights into the importance of data-driven decision-making in driving business growth and enhancing customer satisfaction.

Driven by a curiosity to delve deeper into the stories behind the numbers, I transitioned into the field of data analysis to harness the power of data in informing strategic decisions. Leveraging my background in sales, I bring a unique perspective to data analysis, combining quantitative analysis with a keen understanding of business objectives and customer needs.

With a solid foundation in data analysis techniques and programming languages such as Python, SQL, and R, I am equipped to extract actionable insights from complex datasets and translate them into tangible business outcomes. From analyzing sales trends and forecasting demand to optimizing marketing strategies and improving operational efficiency, I am passionate about leveraging data to drive innovation and drive sustainable growth.

As a Junior Data Analyst, I am excited to merge my analytical skills with my sales expertise to contribute to the success of your organization. With a collaborative mindset, a thirst for knowledge, and a drive to make a meaningful impact, I am eager to embark on this data-driven journey and deliver actionable insights that drive positive change.

Have fun in browsing around and thank you for visiting!
