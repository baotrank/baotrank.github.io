Salary trends for Data Analysts spanning from 2020 to 2024

I begin this project by examining the entirety of the Data Science job market.

The exploration of salary trends for Data Analysts between 2020 and 2024 highlights the predominance of mid-senior and senior roles, coupled with a nearly universal demand for full-time employment.

The data as well suggests a clustering of job opportunities within medium to large-sized companies, where USD predominates as the primary currency for compensation.

A more detailed examination reveals that Data Analyst positions are particularly coveted in the realm of Data Science - with their average salaries experiencing steady yearly increases, directly correlated with rising levels of experience.
